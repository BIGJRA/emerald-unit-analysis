Torchic (Japanese: \xe3\x82\xa2\xe3\x83\x81\xe3\x83\xa3\xe3\x83\xa2 Achamo) is a Fire-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon introduced in Generation III.\n

It evolves into Combusken starting at level 16, which evolves into Blaziken starting at level 36.\n

Along with Treecko and Mudkip, Torchic is one of three starter Pok\xc3\xa9mon of Hoenn available at the beginning of Pok\xc3\xa9mon Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, Omega Ruby, and Alpha Sapphire.\n

Biology

\n

Torchic is a small, chick Pok\xc3\xa9mon with stubby, downy, yellow wings. Its body is covered with orange feathers and a fluffy coat of down. There is an orange and yellow crest on its head that resembles a flame. Its two thin legs and short beak are a light brownish yellow, and the feet have three toes in front and one in the back. A male Torchic will have a small black speck on its rear that is not present in females.\n

Within Torchic\'s belly, this Pok\xc3\xa9mon has a flame sac filled with a burning fire. This internal flame causes Torchic to feel warm if hugged. The warmth feels similar to a warm water bottle. As long as Torchic is alive, the internal flame will burn continuously. When attacked, it will shoot fireballs of 1,800 \xc2\xb0F (1,000 \xc2\xb0C) at its foes, leaving them scorched black. It dislikes darkness, as it cannot see its surroundings. Torchic will follow its Trainer with unsteady steps. While Torchic is rarely found in the wild, it is known to inhabit grasslands. It has been recently seen living in the Terarium of Blueberry Academy.\n

Major appearances

\n
May\'s Torchic
\n

May started her Pok\xc3\xa9mon journey with a Torchic that she obtained in Get the Show on the Road!. It evolved into a Combusken in A Shroomish Skirmish, and later into a Blaziken in Once More with Reeling!.\n

\n
Other
\n

In Having a Wailord of a Time, Ash, Max, and Brock were given the task of watching over a Torchic and the rest of the Hoenn starter Pok\xc3\xa9mon for Nurse Joy while May was practicing with her Beautifly. Torchic managed to evolve into a Combusken and had to be hidden. However, Nurse Joy eventually found out about this and allowed it to stay at the Pok\xc3\xa9mon Center.\n

In Pikachu and the Pok\xc3\xa9mon Music Squad, a Torchic fought with Meowth and Chespin over a Pecha Berry.\n

Trivia

\n
Concept Art of Torchic
\n
  • Concept art from Pok\xc3\xa9mon Ruby and Sapphire portrays a different Torchic design, with large floppy ears and a flame-shaped marking on the back of its head.

Origin

\n

Torchic is based on a chicken hatchling. In particular, Torchic\'s bright orange coloration is based on painted chicks, which are chicks whose feathers are dyed with paint and are typically sold during festivals and fairs.[1] It may also be specifically based on the basan, a legendary fire-breathing chicken in Japanese folklore.\n

Torchic\'s gender difference may be based on vent sexing, a chick sexing technique that originated in Japan, where feces is squeezed out of the chick in order to open its cloaca, allowing the chick sexer to observe whether it has a bump inside its cloaca. Normally only male chicks have this bump.\n

\n

Name origin

\n

Torchic may be a combination of torch and chick (a baby bird).\n

Achamo may be a combination of \xe8\xb5\xa4\xe3\x81\xa1\xe3\x82\x83\xe3\x82\x93 akachan (baby) and \xe8\xbb\x8d\xe9\xb6\x8f shamo (a breed of Japanese chicken once raised for fighting).\n
