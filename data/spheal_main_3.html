Biology

\n

Spheal is a chubby, spherical, seal-like Pok\xc3\xa9mon with a short, flat tail. Its plushy fur is light blue with several white spots of various sizes on its back. This covering of fluffy fur, combined with the layer of blubber underneath, retains heat and protects Spheal from the cold. It has black, circular eyes; small tusks protruding from its upper jaw; and small, rounded ears. Its stubby flippers and its underside are both beige.\n

When it is happy or cheerful, such as when eating in a group, this Pok\xc3\xa9mon will clap its fins. The mealtimes of Spheal have been described as noisy as a result. Because its body is poorly shaped for swimming and can only walk slowly, it moves primarily by rolling across ice floes. This Pok\xc3\xa9mon lives in and around the sea. Spheal also lives on any drift ice that comes near the shore in order to avoid predators. Spheal are often juggled by its evolved form Sealeo, which does so to check the former\'s health. When a Spheal finds prey while drifting at sea, it will inform the Walrein leader of its herd on where to find the prey.\n

Prior to Generation VI, Ice Ball was its signature move, but other Pok\xc3\xa9mon could still inherit it via breeding.\n

Major appearances

\n

Spheal debuted in The Spheal of Approval, under the ownership of Marius. It was used to deal with Ash and Max when he assumed that they were thieves, and it battled Pikachu.\n

In The Great Eight Fate!, a Spheal was part of Juan\'s Sootopolis City Water Exhibit. It and Juan\'s other Pok\xc3\xa9mon were captured by Team Rocket, but they all worked together to escape.\n

In PK19, a Spheal was living on an icy island. It got stuck in the snow, but it was rescued by Pikachu and his friends, after which it joined them.\n

In Leading a Stray!, multiple Spheal helped Luxio free a Wailmer trapped in the sewers.\n

\n

Minor appearances

\n

In A Double Dilemma, a Spheal was living in North Petalburg.\n

A Spheal appeared in A Fan with a Plan.\n

In Destiny Deoxys, Tory Lund encountered and petted one of multiple Spheal. The pod was then startled by the crash-landing of an asteroid containing two Deoxys.\n

A Coordinator\'s Spheal appeared in Mean With Envy.\n

A Spheal appeared in The Champ Twins!.\n

In The Rise of Darkrai, Darkrai hit a Spheal with Dark Void.\n

Multiple Spheal appeared in Giratina and the Sky Warrior.\n

A Trainer\'s Spheal appeared in The Cuteness Quotient!.\n

A Coordinator\'s Spheal appeared in Reuniting for the First Time!.\n

Trivia

\n

Origin

\n

Spheal may be based on an eared seal and a ball. Sealeo\'s skill of balancing and rolling Spheal on its nose is reminiscent of the common trick performed by real seals with balls.\n

\n

Name origin

\n

Spheal may be a combination of sphere and seal.\n

Tamazarashi may be a combination of \xe7\x90\x83 tama (sphere) and \xe6\xb5\xb7\xe8\xb1\xb9 azarashi (earless seal).\n
