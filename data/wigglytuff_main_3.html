Wigglytuff (Japanese: \xe3\x83\x97\xe3\x82\xaf\xe3\x83\xaa\xe3\x83\xb3 Pukurin) is a dual-type Normal/Fairy Pok\xc3\xa9mon introduced in Generation I. Prior to Generation VI, it was a pure Normal-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon.\n

It evolves from Jigglypuff when exposed to a Moon Stone. It is the final form of Igglybuff.\n

Biology

\n
Wigglytuff floating
\n

Wigglytuff is a balloon-like Pok\xc3\xa9mon with leporine features, a bean-shaped body and stubby arms and legs. There is a fluffy, curled tuft of fur on its head, which is described as perfectly heavenly. It has long, rabbit-like ears with black insides and a slightly lighter color at the tips. Its large, blue eyes are covered in a layer of tears that quickly washes away any debris. It is covered in pink fur with a white belly. This fine layer of fur is so soft that those who touch it, including other Wigglytuff, will not want to stop. As a result, Wigglytuff is a highly sought-after Pok\xc3\xa9mon because of its bouncy body and fur. It sheds seasonally, and people gather the fur to spin into yarn. It is proud of its fine and delicate fur.\n

Its body is soft and rubbery, allowing it to inflate by inhaling. Wigglytuff can inflate itself up to 20 times larger, though it has no limits to how far it can inflate in total. While it is inflated, it can move by bouncing or floating. It competes with other Wigglytuff to see which one can inflate itself the most. Should it be caught in a bad mood, Wigglytuff will inflate itself to an enormous size to scare away its opponent. Being the result of evolution via Evolution stone, Wigglytuff is rarely found in the wild, though they can be found in vivid green plains and grassy fields. As mentioned in Pok\xc3\xa9mon Sleep, Wigglytuff is known to stroke its belly while sleeping, possibly to feel its soft fur[1].\n

Major appearances

\n
Wigglytuff (Explorers of Time, Darkness, and Sky)
\n

Guildmaster Wigglytuff appeared in the Pok\xc3\xa9mon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time & Darkness special. He assigned a mission to save Shinx\'s brother to Team Pok\xc3\xa9Pals. Wigglytuff also sometimes falls asleep with his eyes open. He reappeared in Pok\xc3\xa9mon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Sky - Beyond Time & Darkness alongside his guild, during the planet\'s paralysis.\n

\n
Nurse Joy\'s Wigglytuff
\n

Wigglytuff belonging to the Nurse Joys of Kalos first appeared in A Battle of Aerial Mobility!. These Nurse Joys use Wigglytuff instead of Chansey and Audino as nurses.\n

\n
Other
\n

In Mewtwo Strikes Back and its remake Mewtwo Strikes Back\xe2\x80\x94Evolution, Neesha\'s Wigglytuff was one of the Pok\xc3\xa9mon that was captured and cloned by Mewtwo. The Wigglytuff clone was seen again in Mewtwo Returns.\n

In Lights, Camera, Quacktion!, Cleavon Schpielbunk cast Wigglytuff as a lead in his movie, "Pok\xc3\xa9mon in Love", alongside Misty\'s Psyduck.\n

In Thinning the Hoard!, Harley used a Wigglytuff along with Cacturne in a two-on-two Contest Battle against May\'s Munchlax and Eevee in the Kanto Grand Festival. Unlike most Wigglytuff, Harley\'s Wigglytuff wears a rather sinister expression, possibly reflecting its Trainer.\n

  • Pok\xc3\xa9mon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time and Explorers of Darkness and Explorers of Sky: Wigglytuff is the head of the main guild in Treasure Town. Trivia

\n
  • Wigglytuff and its pre-evolved forms share their category name with Qwilfish and Drifloon. They are all known as the Balloon Pok\xc3\xa9mon.
    • \n
  • Wigglytuff has the highest base HP stat of all Fairy-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon.
    • \n
  • In its earlier appearances, Wigglytuff\'s white stomach did not always extend above its mouth; however, it always does this in modern appearances.
\n

Origin

\n

Wigglytuff appears to be based on a balloon due to its inflatable body. Its soft fur and singing ability may be based on plush toys that play music to help children fall asleep. Its round shape resembles a powder puff or a cream puff, and its pink color, rubbery skin, and ability to learn Bounce are similar to a rubber ball. It may also be inspired by the mythical moon rabbit due to its rabbit-like ears, Fairy typing (which tends to be associated with the Moon), and the fact that it evolves from Jigglypuff using a Moon Stone.\n

\n

Name origin

\n

Wigglytuff may be a combination of wiggly and tuft (a bunch of hair) or tough.\n

Pukurin may be a combination of \xe3\x81\xb7\xe3\x81\x8f\xe3\x81\xb7\xe3\x81\x8f pukupuku (cute and chubby), \xe3\x81\xb7\xe3\x82\x8a\xe3\x81\xb7\xe3\x82\x8a puripuri (angrily or in a huff; referring to its personality), \xe3\x81\x8f\xe3\x82\x8a\xe3\x81\x8f\xe3\x82\x8a kurikuri (big and round), and \xe3\x83\x97\xe3\x83\xaa\xe3\x83\xb3 purin (pudding; referring to its soft body). It may also involve \xe9\xa2\xa8\xe8\x88\xb9 f\xc5\xabsen (balloon) and \xe8\x86\xa8\xe3\x82\x8c\xe3\x82\x8b fukureru (to become inflated).\n
