Camerupt (Japanese: \xe3\x83\x90\xe3\x82\xaf\xe3\x83\xbc\xe3\x83\x80 Bakuuda) is a dual-type Fire/Ground Pok\xc3\xa9mon introduced in Generation III.\n

It evolves from Numel starting at level 33.\n

Camerupt can Mega Evolve into Mega Camerupt using the Cameruptite\xe2\x80\x8e.\n

Biology

\n

Camerupt is a quadrupedal Pok\xc3\xa9mon that resembles a Bactrian camel. It has two gray volcanoes on its back, which are bigger on a female\'s back than those on a male. It also has a stout body with red shaggy fur and three blue ring-shaped markings on both sides. It has bangs on top of its head, droopy ears, and a tan fur-less muzzle.\n

Its body contains amounts of molten lava of 18,000 \xc2\xb0F (10,000 \xc2\xb0C), which Camerupt voluntarily erupts out of its humps if it is angered. Otherwise, its humps erupt automatically every ten years. As shown in the anime, it can become uncontrollably angry when it is struck in the face. It lives in the craters of volcanoes.\n

\n

Forms

\n

Camerupt can Mega Evolve into Mega Camerupt.\n

As Mega Camerupt, the humps on its back have transformed into one large and very active volcano. Its fur expands and grows to a point where only its hooves can be seen, and its ears and the tufts on its head grow thicker and longer, standing on end. It gains a gray M on the front of its face and loses the three blue rings on its body. Magma is constantly bubbling up from within its body. It has a deep hatred of water or any moisture, with an explosive personality, and a short temper. The volcano on its back erupts constantly in an effort to intimidate its foes.[1]\n

Major appearances

\n

Camerupt made its main series debut in Candid Camerupt!, under the ownership of Vicky Winstrate. Vicky used the Eruption Pok\xc3\xa9mon to battle Ash\'s Pikachu. Once Pikachu struck Camerupt on the nose with Iron Tail, it went on an Eruption rampage. Vito\'s Alakazam used Rain Dance in order to stop Camerupt before it could reach the Winstrate\'s vegetable patch.\n

In Lights, Camerupt, Action!, Elijah used a Camerupt to help carry his filmmaking equipment during his trips to small towns all over Hoenn.\n

In Historical Mystery Tour!, a Camerupt attacked Ash, Dawn, and Barry. However, the crisis was resolved when the trio decided to attack the rings on its side.\n

In Volcanion and the Mechanical Marvel, a Camerupt that can Mega Evolve was under the control of Levi and Cherie\'s Mega Wave. Camerupt was used to battled Ash\'s Noivern and Greninja. It was later freed from their control when Alva\'s Mega Wave Crystal was destroyed, and it escaped.\n

In An Adventure of Mega Proportions!, Goh caught a Camerupt on Mega Island.\n

\n

In Numero Uno Articuno, Noland\'s Camerupt was living at the Battle Factory.\n

Trivia

\n
  • Camerupt\'s Pok\xc3\xa9dex entries for Emerald, HeartGold, and SoulSilver say that the volcanoes on its back erupt every 10 years. However, its Pok\xc3\xa9dex entries from Sapphire, FireRed, LeafGreen, and Alpha Sapphire, as well as dialogue from the Mystery Dungeon series, claim that they erupt whenever it is angry, which is also shown in the anime.
    • \n
  • Camerupt\'s evolutionary family can be seen as a parallel to Sharpedo\'s family. Both of them are dual-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon, both lines have identical base stat totals per evolutionary stage, both of their final evolved forms are used by Team Magma Leader Maxie and Team Aqua Leader Archie, respectively, and both of their final evolved forms can Mega Evolve.
    • \n
  • Camerupt is the only non-Rock-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon that can have the ability Solid Rock.
    • \n
  • Mega Camerupt is tied with Slugma, Torkoal, and Litwick for the lowest base Speed stat of all Fire-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon.
\n

Origin

\n

Camerupt resembles a Bactrian camel (camels with two humps) with volcanoes on its back instead of humps, though it has proportions closer to those of cattle, muskoxen, or yaks. The blue rings on the sides of Camerupt may be based on a carnival game of hitting the target, what enrages it and makes it erupt, as presented in the Historical Mystery Tour! episode of the Pok\xc3\xa9mon anime.\n

Mega Camerupt, in contrast, resembles a dromedary camel (which has only one hump). Mega Camerupt\'s "skirt" of fur resembles that of male wild yaks. The "M" on its forehead may represent Team Magma; the team leader, Maxie, uses a Mega Camerupt in Pok\xc3\xa9mon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire.\n

Name origin

\n

Camerupt may be a combination of camel and erupt.\n

Bakuuda may be a combination of \xe7\x88\x86\xe7\x99\xba bakuhatsu (eruption) and \xe9\xa7\xb1\xe9\xa7\x9d rakuda (camel). It may also involve Bactrian camel.\n
