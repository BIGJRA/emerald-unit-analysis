Biology

\n

Lanturn is a blue piscine Pok\xc3\xa9mon that resembles an anglerfish. It has big, red eyes with a yellow mask-like marking around it and small, light blue pectoral fins. The lower half of its tailfin is yellow and has jagged edges. On its back is a Y-shaped antenna with a spherical orb at each tip; this antenna is a modification of its dorsal fin.\n

Inside its orbs are bacteria that produce light by absorbing Lanturn\'s bodily fluids. It lures prey using the glow, then blinds them by using an intense flash before it swallows them whole. The flash it creates is visible on the surface from a depth of over three miles (five kilometers). This has earned it the nickname The Deep-Sea Star. Lanturn competes with Lumineon when catching its prey Starmie. Frillish and Sharpedo are natural predators of Lanturn. Lanturn inhabits the depths of the ocean.\n

Major appearances

\n

Lanturn debuted in The Mystery is History, under the ownership of Oliver, who often uses it to swim underwater. It would also play with a baby Lugia nicknamed Silver, which it befriended. It reappeared in A Parent Trapped! and A Promise is a Promise.\n

In Just Add Water, Dorian used a Lanturn during his battle against Ash. It went up against Totodile and won with a Spark-Take Down combination.\n

In Lapras of Luxury, Naomi\'s Lanturn used its Supersonic waves to help her locate a group of Lapras by their singing voices.\n

In Pruning a Passel of Pals!, Kyle\'s Lanturn competed in the Wallace Cup. It went up against Ash\'s Buizel in a battle and defeated it.\n

\n

Minor appearances

\n

In The Legend of Thunder!, a Lanturn was swimming in a lake.\n

In Enlighten Up!, multiple Lanturn were living in Lake Slowpoke.\n

In Pok\xc3\xa9mon Heroes: Latios & Latias, a Trainer\'s Lanturn competed in the Tour de Alto Mare.\n

Multiple Lanturn appeared in Destiny Deoxys.\n

In Pok\xc3\xa9mon Ranger and the Temple of the Sea, multiple Lanturn were present during the final showdown with The Phantom and his crew. They briefly reappeared in a montage in The Rise of Darkrai, which chronicled the preceding nine movies.\n

Multiple Lanturn appeared in Kalos, Where Dreams and Adventures Begin!.\n

A Lanturn appeared in a fantasy in Clemont\'s Got a Secret!.\n

In Going for the Gold!, multiple Lanturn were living in the Ambrette Aquarium.\n

In Securing the Future!, a Lanturn joined the rest of Alola in showering Necrozma with light so it could return to its normal form.\n

A Lanturn appeared in Pikachu\'s Exciting Adventure!.\n

A Lanturn appeared in HZ027.\n

    • \n
  • Lanturn and its pre-evolution are the only Electric-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon in the Water 2 Egg Group.

Origin

\n
Bulbanews has an article related to this subject:
On the Origin of Species: Chinchou and Lanturn
\n

Lanturn is based on an anglerfish, more specifically the footballfish, although it shares some similarities with dolphins. It is also possible that Lanturn\'s origin contains a small piece of Apollo, the Greek god of light whose signature animal was the dolphin.\n

\n

Name origin

\n

Lanturn may be a combination of lantern and turn (as in "turn on the light").\n

Lantern is literally lantern.\n
