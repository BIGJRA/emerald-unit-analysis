Biology

\n

Magcargo is a limbless, gastropod mollusk Pok\xc3\xa9mon consisting almost entirely of bright red magma. Its oval eyes are large and yellow with small pupils and extend upward from its head. It has a bulbous snout and a wide mouth, with two drops of magma dangling from each side of its upper jaw. Magcargo\'s back is protected by a gray, spherical shell composed of hardened, but brittle magma, forming irregular lumps and plates. Marcargo can store fire energy in its shell. A streak of fire leaks from the side of its shell. Magcargo\'s lower body is amorphous and covered in bubbles of magma.\n

Magcargo\'s internal body temperature can reach over 18,000 \xc2\xb0F (10,000 \xc2\xb0C), causing it to evaporate water on contact. Flames circulate throughout its body, occasionally spouting through cracks in its shell. While its shell is brittle enough to be shattered with a slight touch, Magcargo can reform its body by dipping itself in magma. It can be found in mountainous regions and has been living in volcano craters for tens of thousands of years. According to one folklore, when the wild Marcargo population suddenly increases, it\'s a sign that a volcano eruption is coming.\n

In the anime

Magcargo in the anime

Major appearances

\n

In Some Like it Hot, Egan was trying to catch a Magcargo but kept failing. However, after he applied Misty\'s strategies, he managed to catch an unusually large Magcargo.\n

In Love, Pok\xc3\xa9mon Style!, Macy\'s Magcargo evolved from her Slugma, off-screen, sometime after battling Ash.\n

In Poetry Commotion!, Flannery\'s Slugma, nicknamed Mag, evolved into a Magcargo. It was the first Pok\xc3\xa9mon used in Flannery\'s Gym battle against Ash. It faced off against his Corphish, but eventually lost despite a hard-fought match.\n

\n

Minor appearances

\n

A Coordinator\'s Magcargo appeared in May, We Harley Drew\'d Ya!.\n

In Another One Gabites the Dust!, a Coordinator\'s Magcargo competed in the Performance Stage of the Chocovine Contest.\n

In Playing the Performance Encore!, a Coordinator\'s Magcargo competed alongside a Stantler in the Battle Stage of the Daybreak Contest.\n

In A Crowning Moment of Truth!, a Trainer\'s Magcargo attended the Wela Fire Festival.\n

In A Little Rocket R & R!, two Trainers\' Magcargo were captured by the Matori Matrix but later freed by Ash and his friends.\n

Trivia

\n
  • Magcargo is the only Pok\xc3\xa9mon to be obtainable in every main Generation III game, if Pok\xc3\xa9mon Colosseum and Pok\xc3\xa9mon XD: Gale of Darkness are to be included. It is also included in every Generation II and Generation IV game, making Pok\xc3\xa9mon Black and White the first main games since its introduction where it cannot be obtained.
    • \n
  • Magcargo has its sprite changed in Crystal from Gold and Silver; in this case, its shell is edited slightly, making it one of few Pok\xc3\xa9mon to have three different sprites in Generation II.
    • \n
  • Magcargo is the only Rock-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon in the Amorphous Egg Group.
    • \n
  • Magcargo and its pre-evolution are the only Fire-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon with a serpentine body.
    • \n
  • In Generation IX, Magcargo is unable to learn High Horsepower via TM, while its pre-evolution can.
\n

Origin

\n

Magcargo appears to be based on a snail, likely Victaphanta atramentaria or Platymma tweediei, with a body of lava and a shell of rock. It may also be based on the Cherufe, mythical evil creatures of rock and magma that inhabit the magma pools found deep within Chilean volcanoes. In legends, they are the main sources of earthquakes in Chile.\n

\n

Name origin

\n

Magcargo and Magcargot may be a combination of magma, escargot (French for snail), and cargo.\n
