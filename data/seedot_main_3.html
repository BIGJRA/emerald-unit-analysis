Biology

\n

Seedot is a small, brown Pok\xc3\xa9mon that resembles an acorn. A beige, mask-like pattern covers its large black eyes, and it has two beige feet. It has a gray "cap" with a short stem on top of its head. The stem is used to hang from tree branches in its forest home. While hanging, this Pok\xc3\xa9mon absorbs moisture and nutrients from the tree until it becomes too heavy and falls to the ground. The more it drinks, the glossier its body becomes. Additionally, Seedot polishes itself with leaves once daily. If it remains still, it can be mistaken for a nut and startles others by moving suddenly. It also uses this strategy to be taken by bird Pok\xc3\xa9mon to expand on its habitat. Pidgey is a natural predator of Seedot.\n

Major appearances

\n

Seedot made its physical debut in Seeing is Believing, where three Seedot were cheered up by Professor Birch\'s knowledge of Pok\xc3\xa9mon.\n

In Jump for Joy!, multiple Seedot were living in an old tree with several Nuzleaf and a Shiftry.\n

In Pinch Healing!, Max tried comforting a Seedot that was under Matt\'s care.\n

In Promoting Healthy Tangrowth!, a Seedot assisted Tangrowth and other Pok\xc3\xa9mon during several attempts to restore a damaged 1,000-year-old tree back to health.\n

\n

Trivia

\n
  • Together with Magikarp, Rattata, Ninjask, Pichu, and Goldeen, Seedot is the easiest Pok\xc3\xa9mon to catch in Pok\xc3\xa9mon Ranger, with only one loop necessary.
    • \n
  • Seedot can be seen as a parallel to Lotad:\n
    • Both of them are Grass-type Generation III Pok\xc3\xa9mon. Seedot is exclusive to Pok\xc3\xa9mon Ruby, while Lotad is exclusive to Pok\xc3\xa9mon Sapphire, with both Pok\xc3\xa9mon found in the same locations in their respective versions.\n
      • Additionally, both Pok\xc3\xa9mon can be found in Diamond, Pearl, and Platinum by using the dual-slot mode, with Seedot requiring Pok\xc3\xa9mon Ruby, and Lotad requiring Pok\xc3\xa9mon Sapphire.
      • \n
    • They are Pok\xc3\xa9mon that are part of a three-stage evolutionary line and need an Evolution stone to reach their fully evolved form.
      • \n
    • They have the same base stat total of 220.
      • \n
    • Both evolutionary families also have an Ability that doubles their Speed depending on the weather, those being Swift Swim and Chlorophyll.
    • \n
  • Seedot is tied with Bellsprout and Sunkern for the lowest base Special Defense stat of all Grass-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon.
\n

Origin

\n

Seedot seems to be based on an acorn, with its cupule resembling a cap.\n

\n

Name origin

\n

Seedot may be a combination of seed and dot or tot (a small child).\n

Taneboh may be a combination of \xe7\xa8\xae tane (seed) and \xe8\xb5\xa4\xe3\x82\x93\xe5\x9d\x8a akanb\xc5\x8d (baby) or \xe5\xb8\xbd\xe5\xad\x90 b\xc5\x8dshi (hat).\n
