Biology

\n
Several Numel pulling a cart
\n

Numel is a yellow, quadrupedal Pok\xc3\xa9mon that resembles a Dromedary camel with a volcano-like hump on its back. The hump is green at the very top and fades out into yellow with small green spots. It contains magma of almost 2,200 \xc2\xb0F (1,200 \xc2\xb0C). A female will have a bigger hump than a male. It has bangs on top of its head, sleepy eyes with brown circles around them, and short, stubby legs.\n

Numel is a very strong Pok\xc3\xa9mon, having served humans for many years with its ability to carry heavy loads up to 220 lbs. (100 kg). However, it slows down when wet and cannot stand when it is hungry. Due to its dull wits, it does not notice when it has been hit. Numel lives on volcanic mountains.\n

Numel in the anime

Major appearances

\n

Multiple Numel made their main series debut in Game Winning Assist!, under the ownership of Julie. Ash and his friends found a meadow full of them and Team Rocket wanted to steal them. In the same episode, James explained how his grandfather bought him some Numel to keep him warm in the winter. It was seen outside of its Pok\xc3\xa9 Ball throughout the entire episode.\n

\n

Minor appearances

\n

Numel debuted in the opening sequence of Jirachi: Wish Maker.\n

A Coordinator\'s Numel appeared in Thinning the Hoard!.\n

In A Crowning Moment of Truth!, four Trainers\' Numel attended the Wela Fire Festival.\n

In A Little Rocket R & R!, two Trainers\' Numel were captured by the Matori Matrix but later freed by Ash and his friends.\n

Trivia

\n
  • Numel\'s evolutionary line may be considered a counterpart of Carvanha\'s evolutionary line. Both of them are dual-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon, both lines have identical base stat totals per evolutionary stage, both of their final evolutions are used by Team Magma Leader Maxie and Team Aqua Leader Archie, respectively, and both of their final evolutions can Mega Evolve.
\n

Origin

\n

Numel appears to be based on a stylized dromedary camel with its single hump fashioned into a dormant volcano. However, Numel\'s barrel-shaped body and simplified extremities may also draw inspiration from a mule or donkey-shaped pi\xc3\xb1ata.\n

\n

Name origin

\n

Numel may be a combination of numb and camel.\n

Donmel may be a combination of \xe9\x88\x8d don (dull, slow, stupid) and camel.\n
