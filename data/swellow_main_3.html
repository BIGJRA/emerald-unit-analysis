Biology

\n

Swellow is a large, dark-blue bird Pok\xc3\xa9mon with a red breast and face. A crest of feathers sweeps back from the top of its head. A blue stripe separates the red markings on its head and chest, and it has a white underside. It has small, narrow eyes, a pointed, yellow beak, and red feet with black talons. Each foot has three forward-facing toes and one pointed backward. Two long, pointed feathers with red tips form its tail and are an indication of good health when standing up straight. Swellow\'s wings taper to a sharp point. An impressive dive-bomber, it will never miss its target. Additionally, it takes great pride in its glossy plumage and often cleans the wings of other Swellow. It searches for warm meadows while in the air. Swellow preys on Wurmple.\n

Major appearances

\n
Ash\'s Swellow
\n

In That\'s Just Swellow, Ash\'s Taillow evolved into a Swellow during the final round of the Crossgate Pok\xc3\xa9Ringer competition.\n

\n
Other
\n

Swellow debuted in Candid Camerupt!. Vito of the Winstrate family used it to secretly protect his family.\n

In Sky High Gym Battle!, Winona used a Shiny Swellow during her Gym battle against Ash. It faced off against Grovyle, which it was able to defeat, but it was later defeated by Ash\'s own Swellow.\n

In the Hoopa\'s Surprise Ring Adventures short Flying in the Air, a Swellow was among the immense Flying-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon summoned by Hoopa to create wind for Ash and Pikachu, but their combined Gust was too much for them and sent them blasting off.\n

\n

Minor appearances

\n

In Pursuing a Lofty Goal!, a Swellow competed in the Squallville Pok\xc3\xa9Ringer. It was used against a Wingull.\n

In A Staravia Is Born!, Team Rocket captured multiple Swellow.\n

Multiple Swellow appeared in Giratina and the Sky Warrior.\n

In Butterfree and Me!, multiple Swellow were living at Wayfarer Island.\n

In Genesect and the Legend Awakened, Mewtwo raced a Swellow.\n

In A Relay in the Sky!, a Trainer\'s Swellow competed in the Pok\xc3\xa9mon Sky Relay.\n

In The Mew from Here!, two Swellow were seen on Faraway Island.\n

Trivia

\n

Origin

\n

Swellow seems to be based on a swallow, specifically the barn swallow or welcome swallow. Its colors and crest are also similar to those of a treeswift. Its predatory behavior and speedy flight may be based on falcons or the swallow-tailed kite. Its Shiny colors and long tail feathers resemble those of the resplendent quetzal.\n

\n

Name origin

\n

Swellow may be a combination of swell (excellent, or to grow larger) and swallow.\n

Ohsubame may be a corruption of \xe5\xa4\xa7\xe7\x87\x95 \xc5\x8dtsubame (large swallow).\n
