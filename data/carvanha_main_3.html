Biology

\n

Carvanha is a fish Pok\xc3\xa9mon with a body covered in tiny, sharp denticles. The top half of its spherical body is blue, while the bottom half is red with a yellow, four-pointed star below its mouth. It has two yellow dorsal fins above its eyes that resemble shark fins, and a matching pelvic fin on its belly. Its pectoral fins are also yellow. The caudal fin is composed of two red spines with a thin yellow membrane stretched between them.\n

It has powerful jaws and razor-sharp teeth that are able to chew through solid steel, rip up boat hulls, and rip wooden boats to pieces. As a result, sailors avoid getting their boats dented by it at all costs. Carvanha has been responsible for the destruction of many boats that come into contact with it. They mercilessly swarm any intruder and prey that invades their territory, but they become timid when alone. It only attacks when in a group of five or more, becoming instantly vicious. Carvanha is drawn towards the scent of blood. It lives in rivers that course through jungles, as well as temperate regions. Each Carvanha school has its own territory. It competes with Basculin for food.\n

Prior to Generation V, Carvanha and its evolved form, Sharpedo, were the only known Pok\xc3\xa9mon that could have Rough Skin as an Ability.\n

Major appearances

\n

Multiple Carvanha debuted in A Corphish Out of Water. When Ash had to take his sick Corphish to a Pok\xc3\xa9mon Center, he had to cross a river that was infested with them.\n

In The Sun, the Scare, the Secret Lair!, James tried to catch a Carvanha with a bazooka-powered net, only for it to bite through the net and escape.\n

In The Cuteness Quotient!, Jessie pulled a Carvanha from the Rocket Prize Master during their scheme to steal the Pok\xc3\xa9mon participating in the Friendship Festival.\n

\n

Minor appearances

\n

In A Three Team Scheme!, Shelly\'s Carvanha was swimming in a lake located underground in a Secret Base built by Keanu.\n

A Carvanha appeared in Whiscash and Ash.\n

A Carvanha appeared in the opening sequence of Destiny Deoxys.\n

In Pinch Healing!, multiple Carvanha were living in Fennel Valley.\n

In Up Close and Personable!, a Carvanha was living in the lake near the Pok\xc3\xa9mon Summer Academy.\n

Multiple Carvanha appeared in Diancie and the Cocoon of Destruction.\n

A Carvanha appeared in Alola to New Adventure!.\n

Two Carvanha appeared in Yo, Ho, Ho! Go, Popplio!, during a fantasy.\n

A Carvanha appeared in Pikachu\'s Exciting Adventure!.\n

Pok\xc3\xa9mon Adventures

\n

Carvanha was one of the Pok\xc3\xa9mon owned by Aqua Admin Amber, first appearing in Lombre Larceny. Amber used Carvanha\'s Rough Skin to wear down Sapphire\'s Torchic and Aron. It was later left behind at the Seafloor Cavern to cover for its Trainer as he made his escape.\n

A Carvanha appeared in PS604, under the ownership of a Team Aqua Grunt.\n

In PS609, a second Matt appears using a Carvanha at Mossdeep City to attack Sapphire to prevent her from stopping Zinnia\'s plan.\n

Trivia

\n
  • Carvanha has the lowest base Defense stat of all Dark-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon, and is tied with Feebas and Solo Form Wishiwashi for the lowest base Defense stat of all Water-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon.\n
    • Carvanha is also tied with Caterpie, Weedle, Magikarp, Igglybuff, Attack Forme Deoxys, and Stonjourner for the lowest base Special Defense stat of all Pok\xc3\xa9mon.
    • \n
  • Carvanha\'s evolutionary line may be considered a counterpart of Numel\'s evolutionary line. Both of them are dual-type Pok\xc3\xa9mon, both lines have identical base stat totals per evolutionary stage, both of their final evolutions are used by Team Aqua Leader Archie and Team Magma Leader Maxie, respectively, and both of their final evolutions can Mega Evolve.
\n

Origin

\n

Carvanha appears to be based on a piranha, with its red underside particularly bringing to mind the red-bellied piranha. The star on its underside may allude to sharks like starspotted smooth-hound or starry smooth-hound.\n

\n

Name origin

\n

Carvanha may be a combination of carve or carnivore and piranha. It may also involve Carcharodon carcharias (scientific name of the great white shark).\n

Kibanha may be a combination of \xe7\x89\x99 kiba (fang) and piranha.\n
